Garden Horizons is a farming simulator on Roblox by Dawn Digital, launched on February 22, 2026. It quickly became one of the platform's most-played farming games, often compared to Grow a Garden but with deeper mechanics. Getting Started

You spawn with a small plot and a few Shillings. Head to Bill's Seed Shop in the center of the map and grab some Carrot Seeds for $20 ‚Äî they're cheap and grow fast. Plant them with the shovel, wait for them to mature, then sell at the Sell Stand. Reinvest into better seeds and gear, and you'll scale up quickly. The game lets you plant up to 300 seeds at once, so there's plenty of room to grow.

Dawn Fruit, Dawnfruit & Pack-Exclusive Seeds

Seeds come in five tiers: Common, Uncommon, Rare, Epic, and Legendary. Commons like Carrot ($20) and Corn ($100) are great for getting started. Uncommons ‚Äî Onion ($200), Strawberry ($800) ‚Äî offer better returns. Rares include Tomato ($4K), Apple ($7K), and Rose ($10K). Epics like Wheat ($12K), Banana ($30K), Plum ($60K), and Potato ($100K) are where profits really pick up. At the top: Cabbage ($150K), Cherry ($1M), and the pack-exclusive Olive. Weather

Weather rotates every five minutes server-wide. Rain gives a 25% growth boost plus Soaked/Flooded mutations. Storm is the best for growth (50% boost) and drops the rare Shocked mutation. Sandstorm offers 20% growth with Sandy mutations. Snowstorm actually slows growth by 15%, but enables Chilled and Snowy. Fog and Starfall keep normal growth speed ‚Äî Starfall is the one you really want for Starstruck, the highest-value mutation at 6.5√ó. Tracking weather and planting around it is one of the best ways to boost your income.

Gear

Molly's Gear Shop has six tools. Watering Can ($5K) manually speeds up growth. Basic Sprinkler ($15K) automates it for 5 minutes. Harvest Bell ($35K) collects all fruits from a plant at once ‚Äî great for renewables. Turbo Sprinkler ($60K) is a stronger 10-minute boost. Favorite Tool ($80K) marks fruits so you don't accidentally pick them before Lush. Super Sprinkler ($100K) is the top-tier option with a 15-minute growth boost. Like seeds, gear restocks every 5 minutes in limited quantities.

Farming Tips

For the best returns: invest in renewable crops (Cherry, Banana, Apple) that keep producing after harvest. Always wait for Lush. Plant during Storm or Starfall for the best mutation chances. Use Sprinklers while good weather is active. Watch for combo opportunities ‚Äî Rain into Sandstorm can trigger Muddy (5.0√ó).
